ABOUT
We are a tiny, excited, not for profit, only for fun label. Any income that is the result of a tape release or project is donated to an organization or cause that we believe in, or it is given to the artists who shared their work and time with us. "Owning" your music is not our vibe. This is a mission we will remain committed to until the end of forever. We just wanna listen to and showcase good music, and we try our best to be ultra mindful about who we work with.

We think few things feel better than connecting with someone over a really great album or song, or even a fragment of a song. We release music that we truly love, and we hope it makes you feel something too.
