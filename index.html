ABOUT
We are a tiny, excited, not for profit, only for fun label. Any income that is the result of a tape release or project is donated to an organization or cause that we believe in, or it is given to the artists who shared their work and time with us. "Owning" your music is not our vibe. This is a mission we will remain committed to until the end of forever.

We think few things feel better than connecting with someone over a really great album or song, or even a fragment of a song, and that's what we really wanna do. We release music that we truly love, and we hope it makes you feel something too.
RELEASES
GOOD FRIENDS VOL. 1: A Compilation for Planned Parenthood | 2016

The first release in our Good Friends benefit compilation series and a fundraiser for Planned Parenthood. Cover art by Clark Jackson ♥

PLAYA CROCANTE by Amor en la isla | 2016

Our very first single-artist release; a high-energy, diverse mix of sounds that draw from surf, punk, pop, and grungy alt-rock from Argentina. Cover art by Nacho Flores Aguirre ♥

GOOD FRIENDS VOL. 2: A Compilation For Planned Parenthood & The ACLU | 2017

The series continues, this time a double benefit for Planned Parenthood and the ACLU. Cover art by Clark Jackson ♥

GOOD FRIENDS VOL. 3: A Compilation for Apicha Community Health Center | 2018

Benefitting Apicha Community Health Center in Manhattan, a wonderful resource for BIPOC, LGBTQIA+, and generally otherized folks. Cover art by David Castillo ♥
