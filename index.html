PUPPY TEETH RECORDS

ABOUT
We are a tiny, excited, not for profit, only for fun label. Any income that is the result of a tape release or project is donated to an organization or cause that we believe in, or it is given to the artists who shared their work and time with us. This is a mission we will remain committed to until the end of time.

Money and the search for external validation often get in the way of all the real stuff, and we're not trying to mess with that. We just wanna listen to and showcase good music, and we try our best to be ultra mindful about who we work with.

We think few things feel better than connecting with someone over a really great album or song, or even a fragment of a song. We release music that we truly love, and we hope it makes you feel something too.
RELEASES

GOOD FRIENDS VOL. 1 - 2016. The first release in our Good Friends benefit compilation series and a fundraiser for Planned Parenthood. Cover art by Clark Jackson!

GOOD FRIENDS VOL. 2 - 2017. The series continues, this time a double benefit for Planned Parenthood and the ACLU. Cover art by Clark Jackson!

AMOR EN LA ISLA: PLAYA CROCANTE - 2017. Our first single-artist release; a high-energy, diverse mix of sounds that draw from surf, punk, pop, and grunge-y alt-rock from Argentina.

GOOD FRIENDS VOL. 3 - 2018. Benefitting Apicha Community Health Center in Manhattan, a wonderful resource for BIPOC, LGBTQIA+, and generally otherized folks. Cover art is a photo of this restaurant.
