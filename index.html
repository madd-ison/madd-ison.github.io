About Us

We are a tiny, excited, not for profit, only for fun label. Any income that is the result of a tape release or project is donated to an organization or cause that we believe in, or it is given to the artists who shared their work and time with us. "Owning" your music doesn't really make sense to us. This is a mission we will remain committed to until the end of time.

We think few things feel better than connecting with someone over a really great album or song, or even a fragment of a song, and that's what we really wanna do. We release music that we truly love, and we hope it makes you feel something too.
